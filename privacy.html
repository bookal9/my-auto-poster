Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use Schedulify.

1. Information we collect

When you use Schedulify, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and TikTok username when you register
  • TikTok account data: Basic profile information and access tokens obtained through TikTok's official OAuth flow, used solely to post content on your behalf
  • Content data: Videos and captions you upload to be scheduled and posted
  • Usage data: Information about how you interact with the Service, such as posting history and schedule settings
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, and device information for security and service improvement purposes

2. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the Schedulify service
  • Post content to your TikTok account at your scheduled times
  • Send you service notifications and error alerts via email
  • Respond to your support requests and inquiries
  • Improve and maintain the security of our platform
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

3. TikTok data and API usage

Schedulify integrates with TikTok's official developer APIs. When you connect your TikTok account:

  • We request only the minimum permissions necessary to post content on your behalf
  • Your TikTok password is never accessed or stored — we use TikTok's secure OAuth 2.0 authorization
  • Access tokens are stored securely and used only to fulfill your scheduled posting requests
  • You can revoke our access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's account settings
  • We comply with TikTok's Platform Policy and Developer Terms of Service

4. Data storage and security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. Your data is stored on secure servers. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

5. Cookies

Schedulify uses cookies and similar technologies to maintain your session, remember your preferences, and analyze how the Service is used. You can control cookie settings through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality of the Service.

6. Third-party services

Schedulify may use trusted third-party services to help operate the platform, such as cloud hosting providers. These third parties are contractually obligated to keep your information confidential and use it only for the purposes of providing their services to us. We do not share your personal data with any third parties beyond what is necessary to operate the Service.

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a relevant data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at developerinfoapplication@gmail.com.

8. Children's privacy

Schedulify is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
